# SRSM V0.87–V0.99 Completion Dossier

This package completes the planned prototype sequence through V0.99.

It does **not** establish that SRSM reproduces General Relativity.

## Bottom line

The sequence reaches several useful effective and quadratic milestones:

- transported lattice variables suppress the V0.86 anomaly;
- a local correction further reduces it;
- projected nonlinear evolution is stable;
- the Lorentzian quadratic tensor sector is ghost-free in the tested surrogate;
- quadratic continuum convergence is second order;
- Newtonian and long-wavelength tensor limits can be reproduced in the effective model.

However, four decisive blockers remain:

1. exact nonlinear first-class closure;
2. projection-free constraint preservation;
3. a uniform nonlinear two-mode count;
4. microscopic derivation of geometry and effective couplings.

Therefore V1.0 is not warranted.

The correct status at V0.99 is:

**promising constrained effective-gravity research program, not a completed theory of gravity.**
